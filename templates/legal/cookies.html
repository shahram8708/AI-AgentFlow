Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device that allow websites to maintain state, improve security, and personalize user experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential: Required for login and session management.
  • Functional: Save preferences such as timezone and UI choices.
  • Analytics: Help us understand product usage trends.
  • Security: Support CSRF and abuse prevention controls.

4. How to Manage Cookies

You can block or delete cookies from browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may break login and workspace functionality.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued usage after changes means you accept the revised policy.
