Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 2024 | Last updated: April 2025

1. Introduction and Data Controller Information

AgentFlow Technologies Pvt. Ltd., Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India, is the data controller for personal data processed through the AgentFlow platform.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect account data, profile data, task inputs and outputs, usage analytics, and device or browser metadata needed to secure and operate the service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We process data to deliver core functionality, improve product quality, manage billing, send service communications, and provide account support.

4. Data Storage and Security

Platform data is stored in PostgreSQL with encrypted at-rest storage. Credential vault data uses AES-256-GCM encryption. Payments are handled through Razorpay with PCI DSS controls. We do not store plaintext credentials.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with service providers required for operations, such as Razorpay for payments, Google Gemini APIs for AI processing, and SMTP providers for email delivery.

6. Data Retention

Output retention follows plan terms. Free plan output retention is 30 days. Account data is deleted within 30 days of approved account deletion requests, except where longer retention is legally required.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, portability, restriction, and objection to processing, subject to applicable law and verification procedures.

8. GDPR Compliance

For users in the EEA, we process data under appropriate lawful bases and support data subject rights. You may lodge complaints with your local supervisory authority.

9. Indian Data Protection

AgentFlow aligns processing practices with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 and related rules as applicable.

10. Cookies

See our Cookie Policy for details on session, authentication, and preference storage behavior.

11. Children's Privacy

AgentFlow is not directed to users under 18 years of age.

12. Changes to Privacy Policy

Material changes are communicated with at least 30 days notice before they take effect.
